# Comment Policy 

<div class="com-pol">

We welcome thoughtful, respectful discussion and encourage readers to share their perspectives. To keep this space positive and useful for everyone, all comments must follow the guidelines below. 

1. Be Respectful 
   Treat others with courtesy. Personal attacks, harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory remarks of any       kind will not be tolerated. 
2. Stay On Topic 
	Please keep comments relevant to the post. Off-topic or spam content may be removed. 
3. No Spam or Self-Promotion 
	Comments that are purely promotional, contain excessive links, or attempt to advertise products or    services will be deleted. 
4. Use Appropriate Language 
	Avoid profanity, offensive language, or content that is abusive or inappropriate. 
5. Respect Privacy 
	Do not share personal or sensitive information about yourself or others. 
6. Moderation Rights 
	We reserve the right to review, edit, or remove any comment at our discretion without notice, especially if it violates this policy. 
7. Constructive Contributions 
	We encourage meaningful, helpful, and constructive input that adds value to the conversation. 

By posting a comment, you agree to abide by this policy. Thank you for helping maintain a respectful and engaging community. 

</div>
