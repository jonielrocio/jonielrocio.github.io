# Comment Policy

<div class="com-pol">

  <p>We welcome thoughtful, respectful discussion and encourage readers to share their perspectives. To keep this space positive and useful for everyone, all comments must follow the guidelines below.</p>

  <div class="st1"><strong>1. Be Respectful</strong></div>

  <p id="a1">Treat others with courtesy. Personal attacks, harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory remarks of any kind will not be tolerated.</p>

  <div class="st1"><strong>2. Stay on Topic</strong></div>

  <p id="a1">Please keep comments relevant to the post. Off-topic or spam content may be removed.</p>

  <div class="st1"><strong>3. No spam or Self-Promotion</strong></div>

  <p id="a1">Comments that are purely promotional, contain excessive links, or attempt to 	advertise products or services will be deleted.</p>

  <div class="st1"><strong>4. Use Appropriate Language</strong></div>

  <p id="a1">Avoid profanity, offensive language, or content that is abusive or inappropriate.</p>

  <div class="st1"><strong>5. Respect Privacy</strong></div>

  <p id="a1">Do not share personal or sensitive information about yourself or others.</p>

  <div class="st1"><strong>6. Moderation Rights</strong></div>

  <p id="a1">We reserve the right to review, edit, or remove any comment at our discretion without notice, especially if it violates this policy.</p>

  <div class="st1"><strong>7. Constructive Contributions</strong></div>

  <p id="a1">We encourage meaningful, helpful, and constructive input that adds value to the conversation.</p>

<p id="pc1">By posting a comment, you agree to abide by this policy. Thank you for helping maintain a respectful and engaging community.</p>

</div>
