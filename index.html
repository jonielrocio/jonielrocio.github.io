Welcome!

Thirty Years, Two Worlds, One Passion

Hi! I'm P.L., and welcome to my blog! I’m an avid hiker and backpacker from NYC and have been an outdoor enthusiast for more than three decades. What started as a weekend hikes slowly turned into a deeper passion.

Through hiking and backpacking, I’ve learned that the outdoors isn't just about reaching summits or covering miles —it’s about the experiences in between. The serene evenings, the morning calm, and the people we meet along the trail.

I built this website to share my stories from throughout the years and beyond, but more importantly, to meet more people along the journey. You can find more about me at the link below.
