Welcome!

Thirty Years, Two Worlds, One Passion

Hey, it's me P.L. forget about what it means. And let's focus on what we have in common. So, to begin with, journey started in my late teens, backpacking through the mountains of the Philippines nearly three decades ago. Since then, I’ve never stopped. What began as a youthful curiosity became a lifetime passion that has shaped who I am today. I've seen trails change from tropical rain forest to high-altitude granite, but the core of the experience remains the same: the quiet of the morning air, the steady rhythm of the hike, and the clarity that comes with every mile. For the past 15 years, I’ve called New York City home. Transitioning from the hustle-buslte of Manila to the concrete canyons of NYC hasn't changed my compass; it has only made the time spent on the trail more meaningful. Whether I’m navigating the city streets or high-altitude granite, I’m always searching for that next trailhead and the clarity that comes with every mile But a trail is always better with company. Whether you're a veteran of the mountains or just looking for your first trailhead, I’m glad you’re here. Join me as I share these stories —let’s see where the trail takes us next."
